Our priests Mpho and Kyle are helping us figure out what it means to walk the talk of Jesus.

We believe there's a place in the story of Christianity for our busy, modern, not-so-sure-about-it-all lives. Since late 2023, we've been gathering in Amsterdam to explore what it means for us to come together in the name of Jesus, and bring our whole selves when we do.

At All Saints, each of us is finding a place to show up with whatever we have today, whether that be stories of hardship or stories of pure joy. We find inspiration, consolation, and pushback in rite and in Scripture.

We wholeheartedly believe that life doesn't stop when we walk into church, and that the church isn't the only place where we practice our faith. Politics, lived experiences, allyship, and current events easily find their way into our conversations. Working through them together is how we remind ourselves we're not alone.

Good to know

  • Our services are in English Although we like to sprinkle in other languages as well
  • Children are welcome No need to call your babysitter, let your kid(s) join ours! We alternate child-centered services and separate children's activities.
  • We're wheelchair accessible Our church is equipped with free seating and as well as ramps and an elevator to make sure you can find your way around.
